Sensei

Sunil Kumar Sharma

Meet Sensei Sunil Sharma, the esteemed head instructor of the Japanese Karate School. With over 18 years of martial arts experience and a deep passion for sharing the art of karate, Sensei Sharma embodies the spirit of discipline, respect, and perseverance. His journey began in Japan, where he trained under renowned masters and earned multiple black belts in various karate styles. Sensei Sharma emphasizes not only the physical aspects of karate but also the importance of mental strength and character development. At the Japanese Karate School, he fosters a supportive and motivating environment, guiding students of all ages and skill levels. Through personalized instruction and engaging classes, Sensei Sharma instills the core values of karate: humility, integrity, and perseverance.
